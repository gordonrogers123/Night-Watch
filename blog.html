  • Are The Blyth Intaglios Extraterrestrials?

    9/2/2021

    Could this be one of the earliest known artistic renditions of an extraterrestrial? These giant desert figures in Blythe, California, known as the Blyth Intaglios, are arguably the most famous geoglyphs in the United States, and just happen to be located in the heart of the E.T. Highway. They are estimated to originate any ware from 900 BCE to 1200 CE, based on radiocarbon dates for the figures.

    I'll admit, it doesn't look like any person I've ever seen. But, then again, I never was much of an artist. Perhaps these ancient ancestors of ours did see visitors from another planet flying overhead. If that were true, it makes sense that they might try to communicate with images only visible from above. Who else would they be trying to communicate with up there?

  • Megaphone to Mars

    8/16/2021

    If you were trying to communicate with extraterrestrials, how would you do it? Radio waves sent into space? A vinyl record carried on a satellite sent beyond our solar system? Maybe drop a letter into The Black Mailbox in the middle of the dessert? How about... a megaphone?.

    Just southeast of Baker, California, resting on a small hill, sits the Mojave Magaphone: a dilapidated cylinder of metal that looks more like a jet engine than a megaphone. Theories about the origins of the megaphone vary wildly; is it an art installation from some unknown sculptor? Or perhaps the military used it during the chemical and atomic weapons testing that started in the 1950's... which many believe are still being conducted today. Whatever your theory is, the answer to its purpose remains a mystery.

    If you decide to take the drive, just know that the area is very. Remote. There may be little or no cell phone reception. There may be no one around for miles. Meaning, if you did need to scream, there may be no one that can hear you. At least... not down here.

  • Pentagon Report Still Hides the Truth

    6/10/2021

    The long awaited Pentagon Report has finally been released, which documents numerous sightings reported by Air Force and Navy pilots who have come in contact with Unidentified Areal Phenomena, or UAPs for short. While the report offers some much needed credibility to the community of true believers, it's still not the smoking gun we were all hoping for.

    Here's what the report tells us: We have video evidence from military aircraft encountering UAPs. We have detailed and corraborated accounts from multiple pilots of these encounters. We have the pentagon openly admitting that these UAPs do in fact exist. We get admission that the sighting that we have been reporting all these years are not from advanced US military technology.

    What we don't get, unfortunately, is any admission from the government that they know what these UAPs are or why they're here. I'll just be honest; I'm sceptical... surprise! There is still more that they are hiding, still more they are not telling us, and still more work to do, true believers.

    Until next time, keep you eyes on the Night Watch.
