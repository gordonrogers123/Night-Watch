  • The UFO Tracker is Live!

    9/15/2021

    Did you ever see something flying in the sky and you didn't know what you were seeing? Unidentified Flying Objects (UFO), or what the military is now calling Unidentified Arial Phenomena (UAP), are often dismissed as private and commercial aircraft, satellites, or even natural phenomena (swamp gas, anyone?). Well now you can rule out arial sightings of terrestrial origin with our UFO Tracker.

    This page asks you to enter your zip-code and will present you with a list of satellites flying overhead. So the next time you see something overhead that you can't explain, give it a try. If there isn't a satellite above you at the time, well... it just might be a UAP.

  • Megaphone to Mars

    8/16/2021

    If you were trying to communicate with extraterrestrials, how would you do it? Radio waves sent into space? A vinyl record carried on a satellite sent beyond our solar system? Maybe drop a letter into The Black Mailbox in the middle of the dessert? How about... a megaphone?.

    Just southeast of Baker, California, resting on a small hill, sits the Mojave Magaphone: a dilapidated cylinder of metal that looks more like a jet engine than a megaphone. Theories about the origins of the megaphone vary wildly; is it an art installation from some unknown sculptor? Or perhaps the military used it during the chemical and atomic weapons testing that started in the 1950's... which many believe are still being conducted today. Whatever your theory is, the answer to its purpose remains a mystery.
