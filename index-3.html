  • The Black Mailbox

    8/4/2021

    In space, no one can hear you scream... because there's no air, of course. There's also nobody listening, or so the government would have you believe.

    The desert feels that way too sometimes; out here in the Mojave Desert, you might be 100 miles from another living soul. That's a lot of space. You might be inclined to believe that no one is listening out here either. But what if I told you, that out on 51 Rd in Nevada – The Extraterrestrial Road as some call it – there stands The Black Mailbox. A mailbox where letters are not bound for the US Mail, but for otherworldly readers instead. Here, true believers come from around the world hoping to communicate with life on other planets.

    If you decide to take the drive, just know that the area is very. Remote. There may be little or no cell phone reception. There may be no one around for miles. Meaning, if you did need to scream, there may be no one that can hear you. At least... not down here.
